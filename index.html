Short Bio

I'm a masters student in the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University. I'm currently on my last semester of the MIIS program (MSc in Intelligent Information Systems) offered by the Language Technologies Institute. My research interests include recommender systems, knowledge extraction, and machine learning applied to real-life problems.

I'm originally from Brazil, but I've spent most of my life living and studying abroad. Countries where I've lived include Brazil, Germany, Mexico, France, China, and USA. I love travelling, reading the classics, and cooking. My favorite sport is rowing.
